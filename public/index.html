Hi! I'm Carolina. A creative business sidekick with a serious love for organization, logistics, and making things run smoothly behind the scenes.


I'm a proud Latina, born in Argentina and raised in the U.S., with deep roots in the creative world. Over the years, I've explored dance, theater, art, music, film, fashion, and marketing. And have always been drawn to spaces full of creative energy. But while others were dreaming up big ideas, I found my passion in the structure that brings those ideas to life.


Producing films is where it all clicked for me. I was the one happily managing permits, coordinating vendors, handling communications, and keeping everything on track, freeing up creatives to do what they do best. At university, I quickly became the go-to for all things admin and coordination, and honestly? I loved it.


Now, I help creative entrepreneurs and teams stay focused on their vision while I handle the details. From admin and client comms to production support and social media.


I believe when creatives have the space to create, magic happens. I'd love to be the one who helps make that happen for you.
